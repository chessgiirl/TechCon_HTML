History

TechCon started in 2010 as a regional meetup for developers and has grown into an international conference focused on practical applications of modern technology. Each year we welcome speakers from top open-source projects, cloud providers, and startups.

Mission

Our mission is to connect technologists, students, and business leaders to accelerate learning, mentoring, and opportunities in the technology sector. We emphasize hands-on workshops, ethical AI, and inclusive developer communities.
