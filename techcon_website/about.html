History

TechCon began as a community meetup in 2010 and has grown into an international conference showcasing technical talks, research, and hands-on workshops. Each year we highlight emerging technologies and practical solutions that impact real-world systems.

Mission

Our mission is to build a collaborative, inclusive space for people to learn and apply technology responsibly. We emphasize mentorship, accessibility, and scalability.
